Coffee all the time.

Coffee is usually served hot,and is a popular drink in many countries.Coffee contains a chemical called caffeine,a mild drug that keeps people awake. To make a drink from coffee beans,the beans must first be specially prepared by drying the beans and then roasting.

About Cofee:

The history of coffee grounds dates back to the tenth century and perhaps earlier, according to a number of reports and legends related to its first use. It is believed that the original home of coffee (not domesticated wild) was from Ethiopia. The earliest proven evidence of drinking coffee or knowing the coffee tree is from the fifteenth century in Sufi monasteries in Yemen. By the sixteenth century it had reached the rest of the Middle East, southern India (Surg), Persia, Turkey, the Horn of Africa, and North Africa. Then coffee spread to the Balkans, Italy and the rest of Europe to Southeast Asia and then to America.
